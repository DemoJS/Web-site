Demo.js Paris

First french JS oriented Demo Party

DemoJS Party is a french demo party focussed only on web technologies (JS, Canvas, SVG, Audio... well everything in the web).

From 2011-07-01 @ 19:30PM to 2011-07-02 @ 23:00PM

For the one who wants to compete the party starts on friday at 7:30PM. For those who just want to learn and party, let's meet saturday afternon beginning at 2:00PM. There will be conferences all afternoon with a vote for the best demos, in the evening at 7:00PM (this is the real uber show)
